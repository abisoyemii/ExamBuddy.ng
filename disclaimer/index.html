⚠️ Beware of Exam Fraud

ExamBuddy Nigeria strongly condemns all forms of examination malpractice including:

  • Purchasing or selling exam questions ("expo") before the exam
  • Impersonation during examinations
  • Bribing exam officials or invigilators
  • Using unauthorised materials during exams

Any website or individual claiming to provide genuine WAEC, JAMB, or NECO questions before exam day is engaged in fraud. Do not be deceived. Exam fraud carries serious legal consequences in Nigeria, including criminal prosecution.
