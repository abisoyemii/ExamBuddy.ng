About OAU Post-UTME

📋

Screening Format

OAU conducts a Computer Based Test post-UTME screening. It typically involves 50 questions across your JAMB subjects, to be completed in 30–45 minutes. Questions are rigorous and test deep understanding.

🎯

OAU Admission Tips

OAU is highly competitive. Focus heavily on your 4 JAMB subjects. OAU questions often go beyond basic recall — expect application, analysis, and inference questions across all subjects.

📊

Aggregate Calculation

OAU weighs JAMB score and Post-UTME score. O'Level results contribute to departmental ranking. All three components are important — especially for competitive courses.
