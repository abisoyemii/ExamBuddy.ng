Official Exam Portals

📝

WAEC

waecdirect.org · waec.org.ng

The West African Examinations Council conducts the SSCE and other examinations across five West African countries. The portal is used for result checking, certificate verification, and private candidate registration.

  • Check WAEC result online
  • Verify WAEC certificate (for employers)
  • Register as a private candidate
  • Download WAEC timetable
💡 Tip: Use waecdirect.org for result checking. Use waec.org.ng for registration and other services.
Visit WAEC Portal →
🎯

JAMB

jamb.org.ng · efacility.jamb.org.ng

The Joint Admissions and Matriculation Board conducts the UTME and Direct Entry examinations for Nigerian university admissions. The portal handles registration, results, and the CAPS admissions process.

  • UTME registration and exam slip
  • Check JAMB result online
  • Accept/decline admission via CAPS
  • Change course or institution
💡 Tip: Use efacility.jamb.org.ng for e-Facility services (change of institution, mock exam) and jamb.org.ng for main registration.
Visit JAMB Portal →
🏕️

NYSC

portal.nysc.gov.ng

The National Youth Service Corps portal is used for mobilisation, call-up letter printing, deployment status tracking, redeployment applications, and certificate verification.

  • Print NYSC call-up letter
  • Check deployment and PPA status
  • Apply for redeployment
  • Access your NYSC dashboard
💡 Tip: Create your account with your matriculation number. Ensure all your personal details match your university records exactly.
Visit NYSC Portal →
📋

NECO

neco.gov.ng

The National Examinations Council portal provides result checking, certificate verification, examination timetable access, and private candidate registration services.

  • Check NECO result online
  • Verify NECO certificate
  • Access NECO timetable
  • Register as private candidate
💡 Tip: You need a scratch card PIN (available from banks and approved outlets) to check your NECO result online.
Visit NECO Portal →
🔧

NABTEB

nabtebnigeria.org

The National Business and Technical Examinations Board conducts technical and vocational examinations. Results are accepted for technical college admissions and some university programmes.

  • Check NABTEB result online
  • Access NBC (National Business Certificate)
  • Certificate verification service
  • Registration information
💡 Tip: NABTEB is the right exam if you attended a technical college or want a vocational qualification.
Visit NABTEB Portal →
🎓

JAMB CAPS

caps.jamb.org.ng

The Central Admissions Processing System (CAPS) is where you accept or decline university admission offers after your UTME. All Nigerian universities route their offers through CAPS.

  • View university admission offers
  • Accept or decline admission
  • Track your admission status
  • Print admission letter
💡 Tip: Check CAPS regularly — offers expire if not accepted within the deadline. You can only accept one offer at a time.
Visit CAPS Portal →

Troubleshooting Portal Issues

The portal isn't loading or keeps timing out+

Nigerian exam portals often experience high traffic during peak periods (result release, registration deadlines). Try: (1) Use Google Chrome on a laptop rather than a phone. (2) Try early in the morning (5–7am) when traffic is lower. (3) Clear your browser cache. (4) Try a different internet connection (switch from Wi-Fi to data or vice versa).

I forgot my portal login details+

All portals have a "Forgot Password" option. Use your registered email address to reset. If you no longer have access to your email, contact the exam body directly via their official phone number or email found on their official website. Never give login details to anyone claiming to help you reset your password.

How do I know if a WAEC/JAMB website is a scam?+

Official WAEC domains are waecdirect.org and waec.org.ng. Official JAMB domain is jamb.org.ng. Official NYSC domain is nysc.gov.ng. Any website claiming to be WAEC or JAMB on a different domain is likely a scam. Never make payments on unofficial sites. When in doubt, contact the exam body's official customer service line.
