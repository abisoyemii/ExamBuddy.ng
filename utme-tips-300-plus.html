🔥 Expert Tips

10 Proven Strategies for 300+

These tips are used by actual high-scoring students. Follow them consistently

1

Master Past Questions (Most Important!)

JAMB recycles 40-50% of past questions yearly. Start with 10 years of UTME questions.

  • Solve each question multiple times
  • Identify patterns and repeated topics
  • Spend 60% of study time here
2

Create a Study Schedule

Sample daily plan (4 hours/day):

  • 6:00-7:30 AM: English + past questions
  • 8:00-10:00 AM: Maths/Science subjects
  • 3:00-5:00 PM: Second subject + practice

Early morning study is most effective!

3

Focus on Weakest Subject First

Identify your hardest subject and allocate more time to it daily. For most students, this is Mathematics.

4

Take Monthly Mock Exams

Benefits: Tests performance under pressure, improves speed, identifies weak areas, builds confidence.

Schedule: First mock in Month 2, then monthly.

5

Learn from Mistakes

Keep an "Error Notebook" for each subject. Write down wrong answers and why you were wrong. Review weekly.

6

Master English (Easiest 80+)

Focus on: Grammar, Comprehension, Vocabulary. Learn 100 commonly tested words. Practice reading daily.

7

Master 80% of Maths Content

Key topics (60-70% of exam): Algebra (20%), Geometry/Trigonometry (25%), Calculus (15%), Statistics (10%).

8

Use Active Recall & Spaced Repetition

Test yourself repeatedly. Review material from 1 week ago. This improves memory retention by 80%.

9

Join Study Groups

Benefits: Accountability, learning from others, discussing tough questions, staying motivated.

10

Mental Preparation

  • Believe you can score 300+
  • Visualize success
  • Get 7-8 hours sleep
  • Exercise daily for stress relief

6-Month UTME Preparation Plan

Structured timeline to maximize your chances of scoring 300+

Month 1

Foundation Building

  • Complete syllabus review for all subjects
  • Buy quality study materials
  • Create study schedule
  • Join study group
Target: Understand core concepts
Month 2

Deep Subject Study

  • 2 hours/day per subject
  • 2-3 past papers per week
  • Identify weak areas
Target: 65% accuracy on past questions
Month 3

Intensive Practice

  • 4-5 past papers per week
  • First full mock exam
  • Focus on weak areas
Target: 70% accuracy
Month 4

Mastery Phase

  • Solve questions at exam speed
  • Second mock exam (target: 280-300)
  • Practice time management
Target: 75% accuracy within time
Month 5

Final Refinement

  • Mixed past questions practice
  • 1-2 final mock exams
  • Comprehensive error review
Target: 290+ on mocks
Month 6

Exam Week

  • Light revision (30 mins/day)
  • Rest and relaxation
  • Plenty of sleep
  • Arrive early to center
Target: Peak performance
