Our Mission

Democratising Exam Success for Every Nigerian Student

Nigeria has over 15 million secondary school and university-entrance students every year. Too many of them fail important exams not because they lack intelligence or effort, but because they lack access to good preparation resources.

Paid tutorial centres, expensive past question booklets, and questionable online "expo" services take advantage of students who just want honest guidance.

ExamBuddy exists to change that. Everything on this platform is free, verified by Nigerian educators, and written with the Nigerian student experience in mind.
