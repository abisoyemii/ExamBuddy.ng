Official Resources & Legitimate Sources

📖 JAMB (Joint Admissions and Matriculation Board)

  • JAMB Official Portal - Registration, exam info, results, and official sample questions
  • JAMB e-Facility - Change institution/course, verify admission status
  • JAMB CAPS Portal - Accept/decline university offers (post-UTME)
  • Sample JAMB Questions - Free sample CBT questions on the official portal

📚 WAEC (West African Examinations Council)

  • WAEC Official Website - Registration, exam timetables, results, syllabus
  • WAEC Direct - Check Results - Check WAEC examination results online
  • WAEC Past Questions - Purchase official past papers from WAEC
  • WAEC Syllabi - Download subject syllabi for your chosen subjects

✏️ NECO (National Examinations Council)

  • NECO Official Website - Registration, exam information, results portal
  • NECO Result Portal - Check NECO examination results
  • NECO Timetable - Download official exam timetable

🏛️ NYSC (National Youth Service Corps)

  • NYSC Official Portal - Registration, posting, redeployment, and service year info
  • NYSC Online Portal - Check posting, mobilization status
  • NYSC Guidelines - Rules, benefits, and service requirements

🌍 IELTS (International English Language Testing System)

  • IELTS Official Website - Register, practice tests, sample papers, results
  • Find Test Centres in Nigeria - Locate IELTS test centres near you
  • IELTS Sample Questions - Free sample questions for all sections

🎓 University Admission Resources

  • University Websites - Visit individual university admission portals for specific requirements
