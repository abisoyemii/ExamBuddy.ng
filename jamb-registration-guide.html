🎯 Registration Steps

9-Step JAMB Registration Process

Follow these steps carefully to complete your UTME registration successfully

1

Create Your JAMB Account

What to do:

  1. Visit www.jamb.org.ng
  2. Click "Register" or "New Registration"
  3. Enter your email address
  4. Create a strong password
  5. Click "Create Account"
  6. Verify your email
⚠️ Pro Tip: Use an email you check regularly for important updates.
2

Complete Personal Information

Fill in your personal details accurately:

  • Full Name (as on birth certificate)
  • Date of Birth
  • Phone Number
  • State of Origin & LGA
  • Residential Address
  • Religion
✓ Important: Double-check all information. Mistakes can affect admission.
3

Enter O-Level Results

This step is crucial for eligibility:

  1. Go to "Education" section
  2. Select exam body (WAEC/NECO/NABTEB)
  3. Enter registration number & year
  4. Input five best subjects with grades
  5. Ensure 5 credits including Maths & English
❌ If requirements not met: Registration will be rejected.
4

Upload Passport Photo

Photo requirements:

  • Clear, recent color photo
  • White/light background
  • Size: 20KB - 100KB
  • Format: JPEG or PNG
  • Dimensions: 300x300px minimum
💡 Tip: Use good lighting or visit a studio for digital copy.
5

Select Exam Center

Choose your preferred test location:

  • JAMB provides approved centers nationwide
  • Select preferred state/city
  • Choose center close to home
  • Note center code and address
💡 Tip: Select nearby center to avoid travel stress.
6

Choose UTME Subjects

Select four subjects for your program:

Medicine/Pharmacy:
English, Biology, Chemistry, Physics
Law:
English, Government, History, Literature
Engineering:
English, Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry
Education:
English, Education, any two subjects
Social Sciences:
English, Government, Economics, History
⚠️ Important: Match subjects to your desired program requirements.
7

Make Payment

Pay the ₦4,700 registration fee:

  1. Complete all sections first
  2. Click "Proceed to Payment"
  3. Choose payment method:
    • Bank Transfer
    • Debit Card (Visa/Mastercard)
    • Mobile Payment
  4. Follow payment prompts
  5. Save receipt
✓ Confirmation: You'll receive email with JAMB registration number.
8

Book Exam Date & Time

Schedule your UTME after payment:

  1. Log in to your account
  2. Click "Book Exam"
  3. Select preferred date
  4. Select time slot
  5. Confirm booking
  6. Print admission letter
📅 Exam Dates: Typically May-June. Check official website.
9

Download Admission Letter

Your final document contains:

  • JAMB registration number
  • Exam center and date
  • Selected subjects
  • Required for post-UTME
🖨️ Important: Print and keep safe for admission process.

❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about JAMB registration

Q: What if I forget my password?

A: Click "Forgot Password" on login page, enter your email, and follow reset instructions.

Q: Can I change my exam center after registration?

A: Yes, up to one week before exam. Log in and update your preference.

Q: What if my O-level result doesn't appear?

A: Verify exam body, registration number, and year. Contact JAMB support if issues persist.

Q: Can I register from my phone?

A: Yes, the website is mobile-friendly, but photo upload is easier on computer.

Q: When will UTME results be released?

A: Typically 3-4 weeks after exam. Results come via email and account dashboard.
