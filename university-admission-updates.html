🔍 How to Check Your Admission Status

Follow these steps to check if you've been offered admission:

  1. Visit JAMB CAPS: Go to JAMB eFacility Portal
  2. Log in with your JAMB registration number and password
  3. Check "Check Admission Status" under the CAPS menu
  4. Accept or Reject admission offer within 72 hours

❓ What does "Admission in Progress" mean?

This means your admission is being processed. It usually changes to "Congratulations" within 24-48 hours. If it stays this way for more than 3 days, contact your university directly.

📞 What to Do When You See Your Name

Congratulations! 🎉 Here's what to do immediately:

  • ✅ Accept the admission on JAMB CAPS within 72 hours
  • ✅ Download and print your JAMB admission letter
  • ✅ Visit your university's admission portal
  • ✅ Prepare all required documents (see checklist below)
  • ✅ Pay acceptance fee (usually ₦5,000 - ₦10,000)

📋 Documents Needed for Registration

Most universities require these documents. Check your school's specific requirements:

  • Original JAMB result slip
  • Birth certificate (original + photocopy)
  • O'Level results (WAEC/NECO) - original + photocopies
  • Local government identification letter
  • Medical certificate of fitness
  • Passport photographs (8-12 copies)
  • Admission letter from JAMB
  • School fees payment receipt

💰 School Fees & Payment Information

School fees vary by university and course. Here's a general range:

  • Federal Universities: ₦45,000 - ₦75,000 per session
  • State Universities: ₦100,000 - ₦200,000 per session
  • Private Universities: ₦300,000 - ₦1,000,000 per session

Payment Methods: Online portals, bank transfers, or designated bank branches. Always keep receipts!

🚨 What If You're Not on the Merit List?

Don't panic! Most students get admitted through supplementary lists. Here's what to do:

  1. Wait for supplementary lists (usually 2-4 weeks after merit lists)
  2. Check multiple universities you applied to
  3. Consider change of course/institution on JAMB CAPS
  4. Prepare for next year's JAMB if needed
  5. Explore polytechnics or colleges as alternatives

🎓 Post-Admission Steps

Once admitted, complete these steps:

  1. Medical examination at university health center
  2. Departmental registration with your faculty
  3. Student ID card collection
  4. Hostel accommodation (if applying)
  5. Orientation program attendance
  6. Course registration for first semester
