Before You Contact Us

Common Questions

Can ExamBuddy help me check my WAEC or JAMB result?+

No. ExamBuddy is an independent information website. We cannot access any exam results. To check your WAEC result, visit waecdirect.org. To check your JAMB result, visit jamb.org.ng. See our Exam Portals page for all official links.

I found incorrect information on your site. How do I report it?+

Thank you! We take accuracy very seriously. Please use the contact form above and select "Report Incorrect Information" as the subject. Tell us which page, what the error is, and what you believe the correct information to be. We will verify and update it quickly.

Can I advertise on ExamBuddy?+

Yes, ExamBuddy accepts advertising from education-related businesses, legitimate exam preparation services, and student-relevant products. Email ads@exambuddy.ng with details about your brand and what you're looking for. We do not accept advertising from exam fraud/expo services.
