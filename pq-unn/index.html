About UNN Post-UTME

📋

Screening Format

UNN conducts a Computer Based Test. 50 questions from your JAMB subjects, 30–45 minutes. Questions are objective and test conceptual understanding across all subjects.

📊

Aggregate Score

UNN weighs JAMB score, Post-UTME score, and O'Level results. All three components contribute to your admission ranking. Aim for 180+ JAMB and 60%+ in Post-UTME.

🎯

Preparation Strategy

UNN questions are well-structured. Practise your 4 JAMB subjects deeply. Past questions patterns show a focus on applied knowledge and multi-step problems.

🔗

Check registration dates, print exam slips and view admission status on the UNN portal.
