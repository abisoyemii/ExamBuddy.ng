About UNILAG Post-UTME

📋

Screening Format

UNILAG conducts a Computer Based Screening test. It covers the same subjects as your JAMB combination. Duration is typically 30–45 minutes with 50–60 questions.

📊

How Admission is Calculated

UNILAG uses a weighted aggregate of your JAMB score (50–60%) and Post-UTME score (40–50%). O'Level grades also contribute to departmental ranking.

🎯

Preparation Tips

Focus on your JAMB subjects — that is what UNILAG tests. Practice past questions, work on speed, and review your weakest subject daily. UNILAG questions are typically harder than JAMB.

🔗

Official Portal

Check your screening date, print your exam slip, and accept your admission on the official UNILAG and JAMB CAPS portals.
