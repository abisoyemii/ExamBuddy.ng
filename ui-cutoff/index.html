💡 JAMB Score + Post-UTME + O'Level = Admission

Meeting the JAMB cut-off only qualifies you for Post-UTME screening. Your final admission score combines all three. Strong O'Level results (especially A's and B's in relevant subjects) can significantly boost your aggregate.
